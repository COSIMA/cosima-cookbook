COSIMA Cookbook
===============

Ocean and ice models are evaluated and compared by using diagnostics.
`COSIMA recipes <http://cosima-recipes.readthedocs.io/>`_ is a collection of 
diagnostics by the `COSIMA <http://cosima.org.au>`_ community.

The recipes rely on infrastructure that is provided by the COSIMA Cookbook.
The cookbook is under active development on
Github: `COSIMA-Cookbook <https://github.com/COSIMA/cosima-cookbook>`_
